Minimal Group Embeddings

During the academic 2023 to 2024 I worked along side Dr. Chiriac on classifying groups of order 1 to 15 and to find the Symmetric groups of least order that each classified group of order 1 to 15 could be embedded into, this is what refer to as a Minimal Embedding. This project was in partial fufilment of the Mathematics Honors Degree.
